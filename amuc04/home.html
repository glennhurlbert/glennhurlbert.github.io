ASU   MATHEMATICS   IS   RUNNING   AMUC   !!

WHAT?   The First Annual Arizona Mathematics Undergraduate Conference (AMUC)

WHEN?   February 27-29, 2004

WHERE?   Arizona State University (actually the Holiday Inn on the Southeast corner of campus)

WHO?   Undergraduates in Arizona and the greater Southwest (and their faculty sponsors)


CONFERENCE FORMAT.   Registration will begin on Friday afternoon and will be followed by the opening dinner and an invited address. All lectures and meals, unless otherwise noted, will take place at the Holiday Inn. Saturday and Sunday morning will be filled by 15-minute presentations by students, most likely in parallel sessions, followed by a problem session before the Conference Banquet. If participation is high we may also consider having a poster session. Other invited speakers will also enlighten us both days. The Conference will close after lunch on Sunday.
