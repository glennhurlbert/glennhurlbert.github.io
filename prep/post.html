I will post materials here for participants to use after the workshop. The intention is for everyone to stay in contact with each other about content and course issues as they prepare to offer a course in LO, or otherwise incorporate LO material into existing courses, at their home school. By that time we will have developed a culture of sharing suggestions and ideas, so that my role will be that of a moderator, stepping in with comments, encouragement, and "answers" when necessary.

Hopefully, several participants will be interested in speaking at regional or national MAA meetings and also the Moore Legacy conference in Texas about their experiences.

At the end of the year each participant should contribute a short report that outlines their experiences.
