Schedule

Each day (Wednesday, June 25 through Sunday, June 29) will begin at 8:30 and end at 4:30. Hopefully, that's enough time in the morning for a run and some breakfast, and enough time in the evening for some dinner and relaxation.

Gatherings will consist of a mixture of lectures, individual explorations, and group work, intended to be highly interactive, with our final Sunday meeting including presentations by participants. Many of the explorations will involve Maple and WebSim software, a web-based application I developed with one of my former students for teaching and learning the Simplex algorithm, among other things.

We intend to enjoy meals together in general, but will leave some time on Friday afternoon and evening for people to visit various attractions on their own or in groups.
