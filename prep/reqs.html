Requirements

Participants will need to bring their own laptop (no specified operating system), with Maple already loaded. We plan to have it set up so that, if you don't already own a copy, you can purchase one at a discount with a special code we'll get. I'll provide more information about this later.

Participants should also bring an eagerness for teaching a linear optimization course (or at least incorporating material into other courses) at their home institution (highest consideration for those with a support letter from their chair), as well as a willingness to wrestle with alternative teaching methods.

A zest for Ethiopian food is a near must, along with an affection for the blues.
