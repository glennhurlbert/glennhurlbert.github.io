Housing

We will all be staying in one of the dorms on the George Washington University Foggy Bottom campus (either International House (aka I-House), 2201 Virginia Ave. NW, or Crawford Hall, 2119 H Street NW), a few blocks from the MAA. High speed internet access is available, unless you had your heart set on low speed. Additional information on GW housing (and nice map ) is available here.

Walking directions (about 25 minutes) from the dorm to the conference center can be obtained from Mapquest, but we will provide participants with directions and a map as part of their packet, provided at check-in to the dorm.

One can also use the metro (about 30 minutes) from the Foggy Bottom station (2301 I Street NW) to Dupont Circle for $1.35 (buy farecards inside any station): Take the orange line towards New Carrolton or the blue line towards Largo Town Center. Then ride 3 stops until the Metro Center and change to the red line (upstairs) towards Shady Grove. From Dupont Circle, the conference center is just a few blocks away at the corner of the 18th Street and Q Street.

Our group is scheduled to check in on June 24 and check out on June 29.
